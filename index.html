Hi!

I am an applied mathematician working as a stellarator computational scientist at Renaissance Fusion.

Before that, I spent 2 years full remote as an R&D engineer developing algorithms in python at OSE Engineering, and 2 years as a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Zurich. I completed my PhD in 2019 with Bruno Després and Martin Campos Pinto at Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions (Sorbonne Université). I worked on electromagnetic wave propagation models, such as resonant Maxwell's equation to model fusion plasma in a tokamak, and Helmholtz equation.

I used to be a member of Images des Mathématiques' monthly press review, participating in the writing and editing. It is a review of press articles from the past month that deal in some way with mathematics, issued on the first of every month. Check it out!
