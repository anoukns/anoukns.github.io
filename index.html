    Francais

I am a postdoc at the University of Zurich since August 2020, in the Computational Mathematics group of Stefan Sauter. I have defended my PhD entitled Variational formulations for resonant Maxwell's equations and problems at corners for wave propagation (manuscript here) on December 9th 2019 at Laboratoire Jacques-Louis Lions. This work was supervised by Bruno Després and Martin Campos Pinto. Here is my CV.

Research interests

  • Corner and cross-point treatment for DDM formulations of Helmholtz equation
    1. Corners and stable optimized domain decomposition methods for the Helmholtz problem, with B. Després and B. Thierry, submitted. [preprint]
  • Resonant Maxwell's equations
    1. A variational formulation for coupled degenerate elliptic equations with different signs, with M. Campos Pinto, P. Ciarlet Jr. and B. Després, IMA Journal of Applied Mathematics (2020). [preprint, article]
    2. A stable formulation of resonant Maxwell's equations in cold plasma, with M. Campos Pinto and B. Després, Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics (2019). [preprint, article]
